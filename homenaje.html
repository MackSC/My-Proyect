STEVE JOBS

The wizard of computer innovation

Most outstanding biography of Steve Jobs.

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California.
  • 1961 - Jobs lived in the small Californian town of Mountain View.
  • 1974 - Jobs was hired as a designer by Atari.
  • 1976 - Steve and Steve Wozniak together founded the company Apple Computer using the money from selling their Volkswagen van.
  • 1977 - Introduced the first consumer computer.
  • 1984 - Released the Macintosh.
  • 1985 - Steve Jobs founded a new computer company, NeXT Computer.
  • 1986 - Pixar Animation Studios was born.
  • 1989 - NeXT Computer launched its first computer.
  • 1996 - Apple buys NeXT computer and Jobs returns to Apple.
  • 1998 - Steve once again revolutionized the computer market with the launch of the IMac.
  • 2001 - Jobs created a pocket player, the Ipod and 2 years later Itunes.
  • 2007 - Introduced the iPhone, the first of the family of high-end smartphones.
  • 2008 - The year of creation of the famous App store.
  • 2010 - Jobs created the Ipad, a hybrid of tablet and mobile phone.
  • 2011 - After a long battle with cancer he dies at his home.
